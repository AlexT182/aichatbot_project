## The Big Shift

Most sales chat tools still break at the exact moment teams need them most:

- Messages come from too many channels,
- Staff answer the same questions repeatedly,
- Product information drifts out of sync,
- Leads get captured late or not at all,
- Admin teams lose visibility across the funnel.

AIChatbot changes that flow.

It combines a public-facing AI assistant, a multi-channel conversation layer, and an operator-side admin workspace for products, knowledge, prompts, leads, analytics, and channel management.

The "wow" is not just that a bot can answer.
The "wow" is that chat, product data, lead flow, and admin control are designed as one system.

> From scattered support inboxes to a real AI sales operating layer.

## Why It Feels Different

1. **Connect** multiple customer channels into one system.
2. **Guide** product Q&A and sales conversations with AI plus live data.
3. **Operate** the business from a structured admin workspace instead of chat chaos.

This repository is a public product-information and support hub for the hosted AIChatbot platform. It does not contain the application source code.

## What You Get

- `AI Sales Assistant` for product discovery, recommendations, and customer Q&A
- `Multi-channel Reach` across web chat, Facebook, Zalo, Telegram, and API-driven workflows
- `Lead Workflow` with capture, scoring, routing, and follow-up visibility
- `Admin Control` for products, knowledge, prompts, rules, channels, billing, analytics, and team management
- `SaaS Shape` with self-service signup, onboarding, and organization-level separation

## Why It Exists

AIChatbot is not trying to be only a chatbot widget.

It exists for a more practical problem:

> "A business needs AI to sell and support in real conversations, but it also needs admin-side control over knowledge, products, leads, and channels."

That is why the product feels closer to an operating layer than a single bot window.

## Why People Remember It

- It turns AI chat into a business workflow, not a gimmick
- It keeps product and prompt control close to the operators
- It gives sales teams a clearer path from conversation to lead
- It makes multi-channel chat feel organized instead of fragmented

## Privacy Model

AIChatbot is a hosted SaaS-style web product.

High-level data behavior:

- Public visitors can browse the site and use supported public chat experiences
- Authenticated admins can manage data according to their assigned roles
- Chat, leads, catalog, and analytics data may be stored server-side to operate the product
- External AI providers may process prompts and content depending on the configured setup

See [docs/PRIVACY.md](docs/PRIVACY.md) for the short public privacy summary.
