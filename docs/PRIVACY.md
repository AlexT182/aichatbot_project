# Privacy Summary

AIChatbot is a hosted web product.

High-level privacy expectations:

- public visitors may browse the site and use enabled public chat flows,
- authenticated admins may access business data according to their roles,
- conversation, lead, catalog, and analytics data may be stored server-side to operate the service,
- configured third-party AI providers may process prompts and content as part of the product workflow.

This repository does not publish:

- application source code,
- customer conversation exports,
- private business data,
- infrastructure secrets,
- admin credentials.

For privacy-sensitive questions, contact:

- `admin@alphabot.vn`
