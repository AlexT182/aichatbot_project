# Support

AIChatbot is a hosted product and is supported through direct operator contact.

## Fastest contact path

- Email: `admin@alphabot.vn`

## What to include when asking for help

Please send:

- the issue in one or two clear sentences,
- the page or flow where it happened,
- whether it affects the public site, chat layer, or admin side,
- your account email if relevant,
- screenshots if the issue is visual,
- the browser or device if the issue is environment-specific.

## What this repo is for

This repository is for:

- product information,
- public FAQ,
- roadmap visibility,
- support routing.

This repository is not the source repository for the live product.

## Recommended support flow

### Public site or chat issues

Use email for:

- login problems,
- signup or onboarding issues,
- chat behavior issues,
- pricing or product questions,
- channel demo questions.

### Admin or operations issues

Use direct email for:

- admin access issues,
- products or knowledge management problems,
- prompt or rules configuration issues,
- lead workflow issues,
- billing or team-management issues.

## Do not send publicly

Do not post or share in public:

- admin credentials,
- private customer data,
- raw lead exports,
- screenshots containing sensitive business data,
- API keys or backend tokens.

For anything sensitive, use email: `admin@alphabot.vn`.

## Response expectations

- Support is human-handled.
- Security- or privacy-sensitive reports should be sent privately.
